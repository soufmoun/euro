## ‚≠ê Money-Saving Pro Tips

### 1. **Museum & Attraction Hacks**
- **Roma Pass:** Calculate carefully - only worth if visiting 2+ museums/day
- **Free first Sundays:** Plan museum-heavy days for first Sunday of month
- **Student discounts:** EU students under 26 often free, others 25-50% off
- **Evening discounts:** Some museums cheaper after 4pm

### 2. **Food & Drink Savings**
- **Eat at the counter:** Coffee/pizza 50% cheaper standing vs seated
- **Aperitivo time:** 6-9pm, drink includes buffet (‚Ç¨8-‚Ç¨12)
- **Picnic:** Buy from markets/fornos, eat in parks/piazzas
- **Avoid:** Restaurants with tourist menus in Piazza Navona/Trevi

### 3. **Transport Tricks**
- **Walk between ancient sites:** Forum-Colosseum-Palatine all walkable
- **Night buses:** Operate when metro closes
- **Bike share:** First 30 minutes discounted
- **Airport transport:** Terravision bus ‚Ç¨6 vs taxi ‚Ç¨48

### 4. **Timing Your Visit**
- **Low season:** November-March (except Christmas/New Year)
- **Shoulder season:** April-May, September-October
- **Avoid:** July-August (hottest, most expensive), Easter week
- **Weekdays:** Cheaper than weekends for accommodation
