## ‚≠ê Money-Saving Pro Tips

### 1. **Museum Savings**
- Buy **Museumkaart** (‚Ç¨75/year) if visiting 5+ museums

### 2. **Food Discounts**
- Supermarkets: **Albert Heijn** for cheap groceries
- "Uit" magazine: Discount coupons for restaurants
- Eat lunch out, cook dinner in (hostel kitchens available)

### 3. **Timing Tips**
- Visit **November-March** for lowest prices (except Christmas)
- Avoid **April** (tulip season) and **summer** for best deals
- Sundays: Many museums have extended hours

### 4. **Local Transport Hacks**
- Walk or bike instead of trams for short distances
- Night buses run all night (same ticket)
- Consider **Amsterdam & Region Travel Ticket** for day trips
