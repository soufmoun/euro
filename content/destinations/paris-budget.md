## ‚≠ê Money-Saving Pro Tips

### 1. **Museum & Attraction Hacks**
- **Paris Museum Pass:** Calculate carefully - only worth if visiting 3+ museums/day
- **Free first Sundays:** Plan museum-heavy days for first Sunday of month

- **Evening discounts:** Many museums cheaper after 4pm or on late nights

- **Picnic:** Buy from markets/boulangeries, eat in parks
- **Avoid:** Restaurants with "Menu Touristique" signs
